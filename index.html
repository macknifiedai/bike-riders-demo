What types of bikes do you carry? ▾

We carry road bikes, mountain bikes, gravel bikes, touring bikes, and e-bikes — all selected for performance and long-distance capability. Something for every type of rider.

Do you build custom bikes? ▾

Absolutely! We specialize in fully custom builds — from handlebars to hubs. Call us at (209) 565-0880 or email charles@macknified.com to start your build.

How does bike fitting work? ▾

Our professional fitting session analyzes your body geometry, flexibility, and riding style to configure your bike for maximum comfort and efficiency. Book by calling us directly.

Do you do same-day repairs? ▾

Yes! We offer same-day tune-ups and repairs for most issues. Call ahead to check availability and we'll get you back on the road fast.

Where are you located? ▾

We're based in Oakland, CA, serving the entire Bay Area. Call (209) 565-0880 or email charles@macknified.com to connect with us directly.
